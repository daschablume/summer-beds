How to Get to Brännö

Travel guide & ferry instructions to Brännö

Some Navigation Tips for Gotheburg and Brännö!

Local traffic is run by Västtrafik, and you can search for journeys their website.

The easiest way to buy a ticket is to download the app Västtrafik To Go (in which you could also search for journeys). A ticket there can be bought with Swish or card. Card might need some verification, not sure exactly what is compatible and not. You might want to try this beforehand. The other option is to buy a physical card at some store (Pressbyrån, 7-Eleven or similar), and charge it with some amount, then blip it at the start of journeys.

To get to Brännö, take a tram or bus to Saltholmen, then a short ferry to the island. The ferry is also run by Västtrafik, and part of the inner zone, so the same cost as travelling in the city.

Which dock to arrive to?

  • Brännö Rödsten. A classic. It runs approximately once an hour from 5:00-1:00, or something like that, and takes 17 minutes.
  • Brännö Husvik is much closer to the cabin. But the ferry only goes there about 6 times a day, and takes 40-50 minutes. If you have luggage you don't want to walk across the island with (30 min walk), and don't mind a nice boat ride through the islands of the Swedish west coast, this could be a better option for you!
